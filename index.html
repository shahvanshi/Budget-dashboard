How Splitwise works here

Since you settle at month end, here's the cleanest workflow:

1
During the month — let Splitwise track group expenses as normal. Don't log anything here yet.
2
At month end — open Splitwise, check what you owe. Note amounts by category.
3
Log here — Quick log → Splitwise tab. Enter your share per category.
4
Pay and move on — your overview reflects your true monthly cost including shared expenses.
